Clinic & Diagnostics

Allergy and autoimmune condition tests.

Allergy tests identify hypersensitivity reactions to external substances by measuring specific IgE antibodies, whereas autoimmune condition tests detect self-directed immune responses, such as the presence of autoantibodies or abnormal cytokine patterns, indicative of the immune system mistakenly attacking the body's own cells and tissues.

Basic blood chemistry tests.

Basic blood chemistry tests assess key parameters like glucose, cholesterol, electrolytes, and liver enzymes to evaluate overall health and organ function.

Lipid panel (Cholesterol test).

A lipid panel, or cholesterol test, measures levels of cholesterol and triglycerides in the blood, aiding in the assessment of cardiovascular health.

Blood tests to check for anemia and other blood cell problems.

Blood tests for anemia and blood cell problems evaluate factors such as hemoglobin and cell counts, offering insights into oxygen-carrying capacity and overall blood health, aiding in the diagnosis of conditions like anemia and blood disorders. These tests are crucial for assessing and monitoring hematological well-being.

Clinic & Diagnostics

Thyroid peroxidase antibodies, Thyroid stimulating hormone (TSH) and Blood sugar (glucose) tests.

Thyroid peroxidase antibodies and Thyroid Stimulating Hormone (TSH) tests are instrumental in evaluating thyroid function, helping diagnose conditions such as Hashimoto's thyroiditis or hyperthyroidism, while blood sugar (glucose) tests monitor levels to assess metabolic health and identify conditions like diabetes. These three tests collectively provide valuable insights into endocrine balance and metabolic regulation, enabling healthcare professionals to tailor interventions and treatment plans for optimal patient care.
